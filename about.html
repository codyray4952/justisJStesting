Where We're From

Fresno, California

Fresno (Spanish for "ash tree") is a city in California, United States, and the county seat of Fresno County. It covers about 112 square miles (290 km2) in the center of the San Joaquin Valley, the southern portion of the California's Central Valley. With a population of 520,159 people, Fresno is the fifth-most populous city in California, the most populous city in the Central Valley, the most populous inland city in California, and the 34th-most populous city in the nation.

How We Found Our Name

Justis was started as a memorial to long time friend and fellow band member Caleb Justis Sellars, who tragically took his own life at the young age of 16. Originally coming together as a way to deal with this loss, we soon came to the realization that we could use our passion for music as a means of spreading awareness for mental illness and suicide prevention.
